## Brand & Style (Royal & Aristocratic)

The **Vanguard Elite (Royal Edition)** design system is crafted for ultra-luxury, high-end, and bespoke gaming gears. It draws inspiration from heritage luxury brands (e.g., Rolex, Astell&Kern, Leica, and custom wooden artisan ateliers).

Key attributes:
* **Aristocratic Palette**: Deep Royal Navy, Alabaster white, rich Imperial Gold accents, and touches of Burgundy Red for status tiers.
* **Classic Serif Typography**: Pairing **Playfair Display** (for headers, providing editorial, high-class prestige) with **Plus Jakarta Sans** (for light, readable body text).
* **Sharp Prestige Geometries**: Minimalist rounded corners (`2px` - `4px` or sharp `0px`) to convey structural integrity, precision craftsmanship, and high value.
